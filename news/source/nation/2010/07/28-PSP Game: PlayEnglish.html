PSP Game: PlayEnglish

The Argentinian studio QB9 has published PlayEnglish, an educative adventure game that helps players both to learn English and develop their language skills while playing with their Sony® PSP.

Juan Pablo Carrano writes:

I’m writing because we want to share with you the cheerfulness of having made the game PlayEnglish using Blender and Gimp for the characters. At the time of the pre-production part of the team who knew Blender, proposed it as a tool for the production of characters because of its speed and efficiency. The good thing is we could break many taboos. And so we wanted to share with the community.

The game is to learn English, as a semi-adventure game, it has a variety of cartoon-style characters. It was released in Spain a few weeks ago. With all that mess we just have recently put the screenshots on the web.
